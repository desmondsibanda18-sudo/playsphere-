PlaySphere earns revenue from advertising (Google AdMob), sponsored tournaments, and VIP subscriptions. 40% of that daily revenue is distributed to players as coin rewards. A casual player doing 5–8 games per day can expect $2–8 per month from games alone. Active players who join tournaments and complete daily challenges can earn $10–30/month. The app is optimized for low-data connections. Games use compressed assets and we offer a low-data mode for connections below 1 Mbps. Some games support offline play with score syncing when you reconnect — perfect for areas with unstable internet.
